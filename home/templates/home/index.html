Who We Are?

Golden Hive is a family run business based in the beautiful countryside of Killashee, Co.Longford. All our products are 100% natural. Our Honey is 100% Raw. Our bees are positioned in the unspoilt beauty of the midlands of Ireland, dedicated to bringing you the best natural products sourced directly from our beehives. We believe in sustainability, quality, and the well-being of our bees and environment.
